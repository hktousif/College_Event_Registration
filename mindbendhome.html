Mind Bend 3.0 [2020]


Mind bend 3.0 [2020] is the biggest tech fest that helps to exhibit the creativity and latent. Talent of the students in varied dimensions of information technology, mind creates a wide variety of discipline.

It is a one day event to be organized on Mar 4th 2020 in IBMR Degree College, students from Different colleges across karnataka pour test their mettle in technical prevents.

It also pefers to the independent body of students who organize this event along with many other social initiatives and outreach programs round the year, mind bend is known for hosting a variety of events that includes competitions, cultural and ice breaking event.

The event provides the student community to develop and showcase their technical process. The activities culminates in a grand one day festival event which attracts people from all over the karnataka including students, academic, corporates.

    Protocols to Follow

    • All participants are required to carry their College ID cards and produce the photograph of same team during registration.
    • Team needs to participate in all the events to qualify for general championship (GC).
    • If any doubts, it must be clarified before the individual event begins.
    • Post event comments and queries shall not be entered and will be considered null and void.
    • No overlapping of participation is allowed in individual events, each team should nominate exclusively for individual events.
    • Team violating the rules of event shall be subjected to disqualification.
    • Judges decision shall be final and binding on all participant.
    • The college deserves the right to make any changes if the situation demands.
    • Any violation, damage to college property is strictly adhered.
    • College will not be responsible towards any unwanted incident happening.
